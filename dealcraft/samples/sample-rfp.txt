## SECTION 1 — ABOUT NORTHBRIDGE BANK PLC

NorthBridge Bank PLC is a mid-sized retail and commercial bank headquartered in London, UK, with operations across 12 countries in Europe and Asia-Pacific. Founded in 1987, NorthBridge serves approximately 4.2 million retail customers and 38,000 corporate clients. The bank reported revenues of GBP 2.1 billion in FY2025.

NorthBridge has experienced significant competitive pressure from digital-native challenger banks such as Monzo, Revolut, and Starling, as well as from larger tier-1 banks accelerating their own digital agendas. Customer satisfaction scores have declined 14% over the past two years, primarily attributed to outdated digital interfaces and slow service resolution times.

The bank's current core banking platform — FinnCore 3.2 — was deployed in 2009 and is approaching end-of-support in Q4 2027. Legacy infrastructure contributes to an average transaction processing time of 4.2 seconds, significantly above the industry benchmark of under 1 second.

---

## SECTION 2 — PURPOSE OF THIS RFP

NorthBridge Bank is seeking proposals from qualified IT services partners to lead and deliver a comprehensive **Digital Transformation Program** spanning three key areas:

1. **Core Banking Platform Modernization** — Migration from FinnCore 3.2 to a modern, cloud-native core banking platform
2. **Customer Experience (CX) Overhaul** — Redesign and rebuild of digital channels (mobile app, internet banking, and API banking)
3. **AI & Data Enablement** — Implementation of an AI-powered decisioning layer for personalization, fraud detection, and intelligent customer service

---

## SECTION 3 — SCOPE OF WORK

### 3.1 Core Banking Modernization
- Assessment and documentation of current FinnCore 3.2 architecture
- Vendor evaluation and recommendation for target core banking platform (e.g., Temenos, Thought Machine, Mambu, or equivalent)
- Data migration strategy and execution — customer records, accounts, transactions, and product data
- Parallel run and cutover planning
- Decommissioning of legacy FinnCore 3.2 environment
- Integration with existing payment rails (SWIFT, SEPA, FPS)

### 3.2 Digital Channel Modernization
- UX research and customer journey redesign
- Mobile banking app rebuild (iOS and Android) — targeting 99.9% uptime SLA
- Internet banking portal redesign — accessibility compliant (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Open Banking API development — PSD2 and UK Open Banking Standards compliant
- Integration with new core banking platform

### 3.3 AI & Data Enablement
- Implementation of a real-time fraud detection model (target: reduce fraud losses by 30%)
- AI-powered customer personalization engine — product recommendations, next best action
- Intelligent virtual assistant (IVA) for customer service — target 40% deflection of contact centre calls
- Unified data platform on cloud — single source of truth for customer 360 view

### 3.4 Program Management & Governance
- End-to-end program management office (PMO)
- Risk and compliance integration (FCA, PRA, GDPR, and Basel III)
- Change management and staff training
- Vendor and third-party management

---

## SECTION 4 — KEY REQUIREMENTS

### 4.1 Mandatory Requirements (Pass/Fail)
- Minimum 10 years of experience in banking technology transformations
- Proven delivery of at least 2 core banking migrations in Europe in the last 5 years
- ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certified
- Active partnerships with at least one tier-1 core banking platform vendor
- Dedicated UK-based delivery team with onshore governance capability
- Compliance expertise: FCA, PRA, GDPR, PSD2, UK Open Banking

### 4.2 Preferred Requirements
- Experience with cloud platforms: AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud
- Existing accelerators or frameworks for core banking migration
- AI/ML capability with proven use cases in banking
- Experience with challenger bank digital models
- DevSecOps and agile delivery methodology
